## Phase Goal

**As a** solo creator, **I want to** see per-platform monetization-risk flags on each clip's metadata, **so that** I can decide before publishing whether a clip needs an edit, without the pipeline ever blocking my export.

<objective>
Deliver the deterministic keyword/topic tier of monetization-risk flagging (MONET-02, MONET-04) end-to-end: a rule-table-driven scorer that reads an editable per-platform YAML ruleset, produces an advisory risk dict per platform carrying a confidence level and last-checked date (MONET-03), and renders that risk block into the existing per-platform metadata `.txt` output — never gating or failing export.

Purpose: Establishes the "rule-table + structured advisory flag, never a gate" pattern that MONET-01 (audio fingerprint, Plan 02) and later phases reuse. This is the cheapest, highest-confidence half of the phase and the one that makes the feature observably real in the metadata file a creator already reads.

Output: `scripts/monetization_risk.py`, `data/monetization_rules.yaml`, a new `MonetizationConfig` dataclass, an extended `metadata.py` risk section, and full unit coverage. Audio fingerprinting (MONET-01) is deliberately NOT here — it needs the external `fpcalc` binary and lands in Plan 02, which merges its flags into this same risk dict shape.
</objective>
