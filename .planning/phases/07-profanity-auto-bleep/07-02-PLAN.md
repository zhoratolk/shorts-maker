<objective>
Add the codebase's first time-windowed (sub-span) audio filter. A new pure builder `build_profanity_mask_filter` emits the live-verified `volume`+`bandreject`+`tremolo` clause gated by an `enable='between(t,...)+...'` timeline expression; `build_audio_filter_chain` grows one optional `profanity_filter` param inserted after `loudnorm`; and `render_clip` reads the new `plan_entry["profanity_spans"]` field and threads the mask through all three ffmpeg command branches.

Purpose: This is the AUDIO-02/AUDIO-03 masking layer — "each identified span gets a quiet overlay tone applied at render time; audio keeps playing (no dead-silence gap); quiet/garbled enough to defeat platform speech-to-text without sounding like an abrupt cut" (D-03: duck + garble, not a beep, not silence). Filter mechanics were live-verified against ffmpeg 8.1.2 on this machine — implementation-ready, not speculative.
Output: extended `scripts/render.py` + `tests/test_render.py`.
</objective>
